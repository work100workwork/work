About Us

Our mission at Mzamomtsha Primary is to promote a love of learning and of life. While we truly believe that knowledge is power, we - as a school - also recognise that it requires a lot more than knowledge to create a generation that will surpass their predecessors. Because of this, we have chosen to nurture our students in the various ways of life. We hope to instill the core values of this school into our learners. Respect, tolerance, inclusion and excellence are values that we have and will always embody and share through our students and staff.

The vision of our school is simple: it is to create lifelong learners with the skills and mindset to pursue their aspirations and contribute to the world.

Our History

The Mzamomtsha Primary School started as a school that was meant to solve the problem of learners having to travel large distances to attend other schools. We joined the community, hoping that we would be able to create an environment of grooming and growth so that our students would be able to grow beyond their current circumstances and become people that are capable of living and loving.

As our school started to grow, our goals started increasing. As all humans, we started becoming greedy: we wanted more and more for our students. Because of our circumstances, we struggled to manage the all of the facilities that were required to teach our learners in the environment, as such we decided that we would overcome this situation by finding new and interesting ways of learning that didn't require money and other such things that we lacked.

In the present, the Mzamomtsha Primary School has finally reached the point where our growth and effect on the community is so large that we need to improve our area of expertise, which is why we plan to open up our school to high school learners. This growth will require large amount of resources which we do not fully have. This is why we ask that any and all people that are willing to donate money or their services, please do. Your help is truly appreciated!
